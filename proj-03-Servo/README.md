- ## To move a Servo:

  <img src="../md_assets/image-20221205023742334.png" alt="image-20221205023742334" style="zoom:40%;" />

  - Jumper Cables * 3, Servo * 1

  - Connect the lines
    1. Orange (Pin)
    2. Red (+)
    3. Brown (-)

  - The wing rotates.
